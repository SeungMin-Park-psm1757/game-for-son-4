# Style Rules

## Use This For

- Dock icons
- Training item art
- Sleep location thumbnails
- Stage props
- Dialogue stickers and portraits
- Future room decor

## Asset Checklist

- Readable silhouette in one second
- Warm highlight and cool shadow
- No pixel blocks or checkerboard texturing
- No more than one accent color family
- Clean edge separation on white cards
- Works at both `64px` and `160px`

## Icon Recipe

1. Start with one hero shape.
2. Add one support shape.
3. Add one charm detail.
4. Add a cast shadow or underside tone.
5. Add a tiny bright highlight.

## Sleep Backdrop Recipe

1. Large sky wash
2. Gentle light source
3. Two environment layers
4. One rest prop cluster
5. One tiny motion cue such as twinkling light, swaying curtain, or drifting glow

## Mobile Rules

- Keep important forms centered.
- Do not place the most detailed area under the speech bubble.
- Avoid text baked into art.
- Favor broad readable contrast over intricate decoration.
